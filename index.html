WA Animal Welfare Transparency Archive

This is an independent public documentation site to track the transparency and enforcement of animal welfare laws in Western Australia. It began with a single Freedom of Information request—and it continues as a shared memory and signal.

Action Log: What’s Been Done So Far

Freedom of Information (FOI)

Submitted to DPIRD on 5 April 2025.

  • Request: Inspection records of Designated Inspectors (2022–2025)
  • Status: Awaiting response (within 45-day statutory limit)
  • Receipt number: 52549500734

Action Log: What’s Been Done So Far

Letters Sent

Sent in April 2025 to request answers and initiate accountability.

  • WA Minister for Agriculture and Food – requesting public disclosure of DI records
  • AWAC (Animal Welfare Advisory Committee) – inquiry on DI oversight and recommendations
  • Local MP and candidates (including AJP, Greens) – candidate positions on welfare transparency
  • Media outlets – including ABC Perth, The West Australian, The Guardian
  • Animal protection organisations – Animals Australia, RSPCA WA, others

Key Issues Identified

  • No published data on Designated Inspector inspections since 2022
  • Legislative reform has not been matched by enforcement transparency
  • AWAC does not publish its recommendations or minutes
  • DPIRD has not included DI outcomes in annual reports

Why This Matters

In a system that claims animals matter, enforcement must be visible. Without public data, we cannot trust the law is alive. This is not a campaign. It is memory. Witness. Accountability.
