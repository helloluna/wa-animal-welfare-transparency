8 May 2025 – Formal Section 26 Refusal Request + Structural Statement

In response to DPIRD’s claim of no documents regarding DI activities (FOI2025-008), a formal Section 26 refusal request was submitted. The public statement titled “The Structural Absence of Accountability in WA’s Animal Welfare Enforcement” included a political timeline and critique.

Recipients:

  • Primary: FOI@dpird.wa.gov.au, animal.welfare@dpird.wa.gov.au, Minister.Jarvis@dpc.wa.gov.au
  • CC (Oversight + Observers): wa@animaljusticeparty.org, contact@animalsaustralia.org, enquiries@dpird.wa.gov.au, complaint@dpird.wa.gov.au, mail@ombudsman.wa.gov.au, foiunit@dpc.wa.gov.au, Jags.Krishnan@mp.wa.gov.au, info@oic.wa.gov.au, office@wa.greens.org.au

This action ensured the systemic opacity was witnessed across institutions, NGOs, and regulators.

Attachments included a visual timeline, source index, and linked public statements.
